SKZOO

SKZOO is a project by Stray Kids, where each member has a cartoon avatar representing them.

The teaser video was released on the 23rd of December, 2020, and the SKZOO version of God's Menu was released on February 2nd, 2021, on Stray Kids' official YouTube channel.

As of currently, only the characters corresponding the members are known.

Bang Chan as a wolf - "Wolf Chan"

Lee Know as a rabbit - "Leebit"

Changbin as a dwaekki (pig/rabbit hybrid) - "Dwaekki"

Hyunjin as a ferret - "Jiniret"

Han as a quokka - "Han Quokka"

Felix as a chick - "BbokAri"

Seungmin as a puppy - "PuppyM"

I.N. as a desert fox - "FoxI.Ny
